About Female Online Quran Tutor Course

The Female Online Quran Tutor course at MA Noor-e-Quran Academy is designed specifically for girls and women, offering personalized Quranic education with qualified female tutors. This course provides a comfortable and supportive learning environment, focusing on Tajweed, recitation, and understanding of the Quran.

Course Objectives

  • Learn Quranic recitation with proper Tajweed under female tutors.
  • Understand the meanings and context of Quranic verses.
  • Build confidence in a female-only learning environment.
  • Receive personalized guidance tailored to individual needs.

Course Details

  • Duration: Flexible, based on student’s pace (typically 6-12 months).
  • Schedule: 30-minute sessions, 5 days a week, 24/7 availability.
  • Mode: Online via Zoom with certified female tutors.
  • Eligibility: Open to girls and women of all ages.
