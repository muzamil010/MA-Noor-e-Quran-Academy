About Hadith Course

The Hadith course at MA Noor-e-Quran Academy focuses on studying authentic sayings and practices of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). Our expert tutors guide students through selected Hadith, their meanings, and their application in daily life, fostering a deeper understanding of Islamic teachings.

Course Objectives

  • Study authentic Hadith from reliable sources like Sahih Bukhari and Sahih Muslim.
  • Understand the meanings and contexts of selected Hadith.
  • Apply Hadith teachings to daily life for spiritual and moral growth.
  • Receive personalized guidance from certified scholars.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6-12 months, based on student’s pace.
  • Schedule: 30-minute sessions, 5 days a week, 24/7 availability.
  • Mode: Online via Zoom with certified tutors.
  • Eligibility: Open to intermediate and advanced learners.
